If you are upgrading from the Pseudo-Static version of Adblock Plus, please be sure you restart your browser to clear ABP resources from memory.

If you are switching from the normal version of Adblock Plus or Adblock Edge please remove those add-ons as they will conflict with Adblock Latitude.

Please remember if you are switching from Adblock Edge (normal or Pseudo-Static) that you will have to reconfigure ABL to your former preferences. If you switched from Adblock Plus no action is required as to your settings.
